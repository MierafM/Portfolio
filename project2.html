Community currency Site

As an active member of DISC NU, I Collaborated on creating a website that collects and manages data on receptacles that collect foreign currency left at airports. The project was made in partnership with a nonprofit: Community Currency and allowed them to track money donated to airports in real-time. The site alerted the nonprofit when receptacles reach their maximum capacity.

languages & libraries used:

React, JS, HTML, CSS. Amazon AWS
